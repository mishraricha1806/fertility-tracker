# Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 3, 2026

Fertility Tracker is designed as a private, on-device cycle and fertility tracking app.

## Data You Enter

The app lets you enter cycle dates, flow, symptoms, mood, cervical mucus, basal body temperature, ovulation test results, pregnancy test results, intercourse logs, height, weight, age, trying-to-conceive duration, prenatal status, reminders, and notes.

## Storage

Your entries are stored locally on your device using app storage. This version does not create an account, upload your logs to a server, sell your data, use advertising identifiers, or share your entries with third parties.

If you delete the app, local app data may be deleted by the operating system.

You can also clear local app data from Settings inside the app.

## Health Guidance

Cycle predictions, fertile-window estimates, BMI categories, and trying-to-conceive prompts are educational estimates only. They are not medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, contraception, or a substitute for care from a qualified clinician.

## Data Sharing

This version does not transmit personal health data to the developer or third parties. App stores and operating systems may collect diagnostics, crash logs, purchase, installation, or device data according to their own policies.

## Children

The app is intended for adults and is not directed to children.

## Security

The app stores data locally on the device. Before using this app for production health data, consider adding stronger protections such as encrypted storage, biometric lock enforcement, account deletion flows if accounts are introduced, and a reviewed security model.

## Changes

This policy may be updated when app features change. Update the effective date and store disclosures when data practices change.
